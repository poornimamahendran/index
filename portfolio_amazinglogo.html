AMAZING APPVENTURE



DESCRIPTION

Amazing AppVenture is a game which were developed in Android platform for both android and iOS.Both the games are something like “Treasure Hunt” games in which questions will be displayed in the screen and the user have to read the question and have to answer. The answer for the question will be a photo,video,written answer or scanning a QR code. The both games can have unlimited teams to play and location of the each team can able to see the current location of the other teams in their respective devices using the Google Map. The main objective of the two games is to fully make fun between the teams who plays the game.
